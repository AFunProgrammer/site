Privacy Policy for Mines

Effective Date: May 22, 2025

Thank you for playing Mines. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our application.

Information We Do Not Collect:

We want to explicitly state that Mines by inspiringfamily.com does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. Furthermore, we do not integrate any third-party services for advertising or analytics. This means we do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data

Third-Party Services:

Mines does not utilize any third-party services for advertising or analytics. Therefore, no data is shared with such services.

Data Security:

Since Mines does not collect any personal information and does not use third-party services for data collection, there is no personal data to store or transmit. We are committed to providing a safe and private gaming experience.

Children's Privacy:

Mines is a family-friendly game and is not specifically directed towards children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy within the app or on our website at our website.
